CHARACTER BIO

Miller, James "Buddy" T.

Basic Information

OCCUPATION: Visual Artist / Creative Support

PRONOUNS: He/Him

GENDER: Male

SEX: Male

AGE: 26

BIRTHDAY: March 15

HEIGHT: 5'10"

BLOOD TYPE: O+

Preferences

LIKES: Art supplies, coffee shops, people-watching, bright colors, sketching during briefings, making everyone laugh, hanging out with his best friend

DISLIKES: Boring meetings, running out of sketch paper, when people don't appreciate his doodles, seeing his best friend stressed

COLOR PALETTE

HAIR: Blonde

EYES: Blue

SKIN: Fair

Additional Notes

Position: Creative Support / Visual Documentation

Summary: The resident artist who somehow ended up in intelligence work. James keeps the operation running with his quick sketches, visual recon, and ability to make even the most serious agents crack a smile. He documents everything in his sketchbook and has a habit of doodling during important briefings—much to his handler's chagrin.

Specialties: Visual documentation, sketch-based reconnaissance, creating cover art for fake identities, lightening the mood when things get too tense

Relationship to Agent: Best friend of the lead agent. The cheerful, slightly chaotic friend who keeps things fun and always has the lead agent's back. Always ready with a joke or a perfectly timed sketch that somehow ends up being useful. Their friendship goes way back, and he's one of the few people the lead agent truly trusts.
